Classic Vanilla Cake Recipe:

Preheat oven to 180°C. Mix 2 cups flour, 1½ tsp baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Cream 1 cup sugar with ½ cup butter, then blend 2 eggs and 1 tsp vanilla. Alternate adding dry mix and ¾ cup milk. Pour into greased pan, bake 30 minutes. Cool, frost.
